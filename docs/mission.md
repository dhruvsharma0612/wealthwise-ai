# Mission

## Mission Statement

> **We help people make confident financial decisions every day by combining simple personal-finance tools, explainable AI guidance, and trustworthy access to human experts — without ever taking control of their money away from them.**

The [Vision](vision.md) describes the destination. The mission describes what WealthWise does *daily* to move toward it.

---

## What we do every day

1. **Make money visible.** Give every user a clear, current picture of their income, spending, savings, goals, and net worth.
2. **Make money understandable.** Use AI and education to explain *why* the numbers look the way they do, in plain language.
3. **Make better decisions easier.** Nudge good habits, surface risks early, and suggest next steps the user can act on.
4. **Make expert help accessible.** Connect users to verified advisors and agencies when they want a human in the loop.
5. **Keep the user in control.** Never execute transactions automatically; never hide the reasoning; never sell out the user's trust.

---

## How the mission shows up in the product

| Mission pillar | How it appears |
|---|---|
| Make money visible | Income, expense, savings, net worth, and dashboard modules |
| Make money understandable | AI Coach explanations, Learning Hub, explainable recommendations |
| Make better decisions easier | Budgeting, goal planning, proactive nudges, reports |
| Make expert help accessible | Advisor Marketplace, Agency Portal, meetings, messaging |
| Keep the user in control | Business rules: AI never transacts, users own their data, everything is explainable |

## Who we serve

WealthWise serves people across the entire financial journey — from a student tracking a first paycheck to a family planning retirement to a business owner separating personal and professional finances — and the **professionals and agencies** who advise them. See [Personas](personas.md) and [Stakeholders](stakeholders.md).

## How we measure whether we are succeeding

The mission is accomplished when users are measurably better off and understand why. Concretely, we track:

- Improvement in users' savings rate and goal completion over time
- Reduction in high-interest debt among active users
- Engagement with explanations and education (understanding, not just usage)
- Advisor and agency activity and client satisfaction
- Retention as a proxy for sustained value

Full detail in [Success Metrics](success-metrics.md).

---

## What we will not do

The mission is defined as much by its boundaries as its goals:

- We will not execute financial transactions on a user's behalf without explicit, human-confirmed authorization.
- We will not give recommendations the user cannot understand or audit.
- We will not monetize by compromising user privacy or trust.
- We will not let AI replace the human advisor relationship — only augment it.

These boundaries are formalized in [Business Rules](business-rules.md) and [Principles](principles.md).
