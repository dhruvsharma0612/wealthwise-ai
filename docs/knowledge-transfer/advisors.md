# Knowledge Transfer: Advisors

> For financial professionals serving clients on WealthWise — independent or within an agency.

## What is WealthWise?
An AI-powered financial ecosystem where people manage money themselves, with AI, or with professionals like you. For you, it's a place to **build a practice, find well-matched clients, and serve them better with less busywork**. See [Advisor Ecosystem](../advisor-ecosystem.md).

## Why does it exist?
Because trustworthy human advice is hard to find and afford, and admin overhead steals time from advising. WealthWise solves discovery and trust for clients, and removes busywork for you — so the human relationship can be its best. See [Vision](../vision.md).

## How do you use it (as an advisor)?
- **Join and get verified** — your credentials are verified before you serve clients; this protects clients *and* your credibility ([BR-12](../business-rules.md#br-12--advisors-and-agencies-are-verified-before-serving-clients)).
- **Build your profile** — specialties, experience, approach, availability; this is how clients find and choose you ([Marketplace](../modules.md#advisor-marketplace)).
- **Serve assigned clients** — you see the data of clients who've granted you access, with full context already in place, so meetings start ahead.
- **Use the tools** — [meetings](../modules.md#meetings), [messaging](../modules.md#messaging), [document management](../modules.md#document-management), reports, and **AI as your assistant** (drafting plans, summarizing, surfacing context — you review and own the output).
- **Build reputation** — genuine [ratings](../advisor-ecosystem.md#ratings) grow your practice.

## What are your responsibilities?
- Provide **quality, explainable, professional** guidance — clients can always ask "why?" ([BR-03](../business-rules.md#br-03--every-recommendation-must-be-explainable)).
- **Protect client trust and privacy** — access only your assigned clients; data isn't yours, it's theirs ([BR-07](../business-rules.md#br-07--users-own-their-financial-data)).
- **Own your AI-assisted work** — AI drafts; you are accountable for what you deliver ([BR-02](../business-rules.md)).
- **Maintain conduct and compliance** — standards are ongoing, not one-time.
- **Respect the client's freedom** — they can switch advisors anytime; serve so well they don't want to.

## What value do you receive?
- A **client pipeline** without expensive prospecting.
- **Less admin, more advising** — AI and platform tools handle busywork.
- **Pre-loaded context** so every client conversation is productive.
- A **transparent reputation** that compounds with good service.

## What WealthWise will NOT do for you
- It won't move money or execute trades on a client's behalf — the platform doesn't transact ([BR-01](../business-rules.md#br-01--ai-never-executes-financial-transactions)).
- It won't let AI replace you — AI is your assistant, not your substitute.
- It won't lock clients in — your edge is service quality, not lock-in.
