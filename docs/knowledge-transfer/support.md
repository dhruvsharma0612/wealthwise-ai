# Knowledge Transfer: Support Team

> For support staff (and compliance-adjacent operations) who help users and keep trust intact.

## What is WealthWise?
An AI-powered financial ecosystem where users manage money themselves, with AI, or with verified human advisors and agencies. You are the human face of the platform's promise of **trust**. See [Platform Overview](../platform-overview.md).

## Why does it exist?
To help people understand and improve their finances without losing control of their money. Every support interaction either reinforces or undermines that trust. See [Mission](../mission.md).

## How do you use it (as support)?
- **Know the modules** so you can guide users through them ([Modules](../modules.md)).
- **Know the business rules** — they're your baseline for what is and isn't allowed:
  - AI never moves money or replaces an advisor ([BR-01](../business-rules.md#br-01--ai-never-executes-financial-transactions), [BR-02](../business-rules.md)).
  - Users own their data and can export it ([BR-07](../business-rules.md#br-07--users-own-their-financial-data), [BR-11](../business-rules.md)).
  - Agencies can't see each other's data ([BR-09](../business-rules.md#br-09--agencies-cannot-access-another-agencys-data)).
- **Know the roles** so you understand who's allowed to do what ([Roles](../roles.md)).
- **Access with restraint:** need-to-know only, and remember access is logged ([BR-08](../business-rules.md)).

## What are your responsibilities?
- **Resolve fast, accurately, and empathetically.** Money issues are emotional; lead with care.
- **Protect privacy** during every interaction — verify identity, share only what's appropriate.
- **Escalate faithfully:** route security, compliance, and complex financial issues to the right team (engineering, compliance, or [Platform Admin/Super Admin](../roles.md)). Critical issues (suspected breach, data-isolation failure, AI overstepping) escalate immediately — these are zero-tolerance ([Risks](../risks.md)).
- **Never make financial decisions for users** or give regulated advice — that's not the support role ([BR-15](../business-rules.md#br-15--education-guidance-and-regulated-advice-are-distinguished)).
- **Report patterns:** recurring issues are product signals; surface them.

## What value do you receive?
- Clear product knowledge and documented rules so you can act with confidence.
- Defined escalation paths so you're never stuck.
- A meaningful role: you are a primary guardian of user trust.

## Quick reference: common "can we…?" answers
| User asks… | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Can the AI just invest this for me?" | No — AI never executes transactions. |
| "Can I take my data and leave?" | Yes — you own your data and can export it. |
| "Can my old advisor still see my info?" | No — switching revokes their access. |
| "Can another agency see my finances?" | Never — strict data isolation. |
| "Why did it recommend this?" | Every recommendation is explainable — show them the reason. |
