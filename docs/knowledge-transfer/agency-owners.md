# Knowledge Transfer: Agency Owners

> For principals running a wealth-management firm on WealthWise.

## What is WealthWise?
An AI-powered financial ecosystem serving individuals and the professionals who advise them. For you, it's a platform to **run and grow a firm** — manage a team of advisors and a book of clients, with oversight, branding, and a viable business model. See [Organization Model](../organization-model.md).

## Why does it exist?
To make trustworthy human advice accessible at scale. Agencies are how WealthWise delivers higher-touch, professional, multi-advisor service — and how firms like yours reach clients and operate efficiently. See [Vision](../vision.md).

## How do you use it (as an agency owner)?
- **Onboard your agency** — apply and get verified ([Advisor Ecosystem](../advisor-ecosystem.md#agency-lifecycle)).
- **Set up the firm** — branding, settings, and structure in the [Agency Portal](../modules.md#agency-portal).
- **Onboard and verify advisors** — build your roster ([Roles](../roles.md)).
- **Assign clients to advisors** — match by specialty, fit, and capacity ([client assignment](../advisor-ecosystem.md#client-assignment)).
- **Oversee quality and compliance** — monitor your advisors and clients, *within your own data only*.
- **Grow the book** — scale advisors and clients on a clear, repeatable structure.

## What are your responsibilities?
- **Quality, compliance, and conduct** of your firm and its advisors.
- **Respect data ownership and isolation:**
  - You and your advisors are **granted access** to client data — clients **own** it ([BR-07](../business-rules.md#br-07--users-own-their-financial-data)).
  - You can **never** access another agency's data, and no other agency can access yours ([BR-09](../business-rules.md#br-09--agencies-cannot-access-another-agencys-data)).
  - Advisors see only **their assigned** clients (unless their role grants oversight).
- **Honor client freedom** — clients can switch advisors or leave with their data; compete on service.
- **Uphold platform standards** — verification, explainability, privacy, and conduct.

## What value do you receive?
- A platform to **run your entire practice**: team management, client management, oversight, branding.
- **Strict data isolation** that protects your firm and your clients.
- **A client channel** and tools (AI assistance, scheduling, messaging, documents) that make advisors more productive.
- A clear **business model** to grow on.

## Boundaries to remember
- The platform never moves money on a client's behalf ([BR-01](../business-rules.md#br-01--ai-never-executes-financial-transactions)).
- AI assists your advisors; it doesn't replace them ([BR-02](../business-rules.md)).
- Ownership of client data always stays with the client.
