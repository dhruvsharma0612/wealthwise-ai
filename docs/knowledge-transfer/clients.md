# Knowledge Transfer: Clients (End Users)

> For the people WealthWise is ultimately for — anyone who wants to understand and improve their money.

## What is WealthWise?
Your financial home. One place to **see, understand, and grow your money** — on your own, with an AI coach, or with verified human advisors and agencies. You always choose how much help you want. It's not just a budgeting app; it's built to support your whole financial life over time. See [Platform Overview](../platform-overview.md).

## Why does it exist?
Because money is stressful, confusing, and the good help is hard to find. WealthWise exists to make your finances **clear**, give you **plain-language guidance**, and connect you to **trustworthy humans** when you want them — without ever taking control of your money away from you. See [Vision](../vision.md).

## How do you use it (as a client)?
You choose your level of help — and can change anytime ([the independence spectrum](../platform-overview.md#the-independence-spectrum)):
1. **Do it yourself** — track income and expenses, set a budget, save toward goals, see your dashboard and reports.
2. **Add AI** — let the [AI Coach](../modules.md#ai-coach) explain your numbers in plain language and coach better habits.
3. **Add a human** — find a verified [advisor](../advisor-ecosystem.md) through the marketplace when you want expert help.
4. **Go full-service** — work with a professional [agency](../organization-model.md) for higher-touch support.

As your life gets more complex, deeper tools (investments, insurance, retirement, tax) become available ([Modules](../modules.md)).

## What are your responsibilities?
- **Provide accurate information** — good guidance depends on a true picture.
- **Make your own decisions** — WealthWise and its advisors help you decide; you stay in control ([BR-17](../business-rules.md#user-control-rules)).
- **Manage who sees your data** — you decide which advisors or agencies get access, and you can revoke it ([BR-08](../business-rules.md)).

## What value do you receive?
- **Clarity** — a clear picture of your money.
- **Understanding** — every recommendation comes with a "why" ([BR-03](../business-rules.md#br-03--every-recommendation-must-be-explainable)).
- **Choice** — help on your terms, from self-service to full-service.
- **Control & ownership** — you own your data and can take it with you ([BR-07](../business-rules.md#br-07--users-own-their-financial-data), [BR-11](../business-rules.md)).
- **Trust** — verified professionals, mandatory privacy, no money moved without you.

## Your guarantees (what we promise you)
- **AI will never move your money or make a transaction for you** ([BR-01](../business-rules.md#br-01--ai-never-executes-financial-transactions)).
- **You own your financial data, always** — and can export it and leave anytime.
- **No one else's agency can see your data; no advisor sees it without your permission.**
- **Everything is explainable** — you can always ask "why?"
- **You're always in control** — nothing financially consequential happens by surprise.
