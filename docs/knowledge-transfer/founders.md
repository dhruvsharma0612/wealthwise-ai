# Knowledge Transfer: Founders & Leadership

> For founders, leadership, and investors who need to explain, sell, and steer WealthWise.

## What is WealthWise?
An **AI-powered financial ecosystem** that helps people manage their money independently, with AI guidance, or with verified human advisors and agencies — always the user's choice. Not just a budgeting app: the long-term ambition is to be the **operating system for personal finance**. See [Vision](../vision.md).

## Why does it exist?
Three gaps: **financial literacy** is poor, **good advice** is expensive and hard to vet, and **AI** can democratize guidance if done responsibly. WealthWise closes all three by combining simple tools, explainable AI, and trustworthy access to humans — without taking control of the user's money. See [Mission](../mission.md).

## How do you use it (as a founder)?
- **Explain it** consistently using the [elevator pitch](../wealthwise-bible.md#elevator-pitch), vision, and mission.
- **Steer it** using the [Roadmap](../roadmap.md) (Phases 0→4) and [Success Metrics](../success-metrics.md) (the North Star).
- **Protect it** by upholding [Principles](../principles.md) and [Business Rules](../business-rules.md) — especially trust, privacy, and "AI never transacts."
- **Defend it** against the [Risks](../risks.md), with trust as the asset you most guard.

## What are your responsibilities?
- Be the keeper of **vision and trust**. Every decision either compounds or spends trust.
- Resolve stakeholder tensions using the principles (growth vs. privacy, AI autonomy vs. safety). See [Stakeholders](../stakeholders.md).
- Make the hard, principled "no" when short-term gain conflicts with long-term trust.
- Ensure the business model is sustainable **without** compromising privacy or user-first values.

## What value do you receive?
- A defensible, trust-based business in a massive market.
- A documented, shared understanding (this `/docs` set) so the team executes coherently.
- A North Star that keeps growth honest: users genuinely better off, and understanding why.

## The 60-second pitch
> "Most people are anxious about money, were never taught how it works, and can't find or afford good advice. WealthWise is the financial home that meets you where you are — track and understand your money yourself, get plain-language AI coaching, or connect with verified human advisors and agencies, all in one trusted place. AI assists; it never moves your money or replaces your advisor. We're building the operating system for personal finance."
