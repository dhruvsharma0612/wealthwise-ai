# Product Roadmap

The roadmap describes **what WealthWise builds, in what order, and why** — at a product level, not a technical one. It is organized into phases that build on each other like layers, consistent with the [Platform Overview](platform-overview.md) rings.

Each phase lists its theme, scope, the value it unlocks, and how we know it's done. Phases are sequential in priority but may overlap in execution.

> **Guiding logic:** Build the trusted foundation first (the user managing their own money with understanding), then add AI guidance, then human help, then deep wealth planning, then open automation. Trust and explainability are present from day one.

## Phase 1 — Personal Finance MVP

**Theme:** The user manages their own money, and understands it.

**Scope:**
- Authentication & User Profiles
- Income tracking
- Expense tracking
- Budgeting
- Savings
- Goal planning
- Dashboard (the unified financial picture)
- **AI Explanation** (the first taste of AI — explaining the user's numbers in plain language)
- Financial reports
- Notifications

**Value:** A person can see, understand, and start improving their finances entirely on their own, with AI explaining what the numbers mean. This proves the core promise: clarity + understanding.

**Done when:** A self-directed user can track income/expenses, set a budget and goals, see a trustworthy dashboard and report, and get plain-language AI explanations of their situation.

---

## Phase 2 — Human Help & AI Coach

**Theme:** Bring in people — AI as a coach, and verified humans.

**Scope:**
- Advisor Marketplace
- Agency Portal
- Advisor profiles & verification
- Messaging
- Appointments / Meetings
- **AI Coach** (from explanations to interactive coaching)
- Foundations of the [Organization Model](organization-model.md) and [Roles](roles.md) in practice

**Value:** Users can move along the [independence spectrum](platform-overview.md#the-independence-spectrum) — get AI coaching, or connect to verified advisors and agencies. The human network and its data-isolation guarantees come to life.

**Done when:** A user can find and connect with a verified advisor/agency, communicate and meet with them, and an AI Coach can interactively guide everyday questions.

---

## Phase 3 — Wealth Planning Depth

**Theme:** Go beyond budgeting into building and protecting wealth.

**Scope:**
- Investment planning (plan & educate; no trade execution)
- Insurance planning
- Retirement planning
- Tax planning
- Loan/debt planning depth
- Family accounts
- Portfolio tracking

**Value:** WealthWise becomes a full financial life platform, not just day-to-day money management — serving families, parents, business owners, and pre-retirees at greater depth, with advisors for complex needs.

**Done when:** Users (and their advisors) can plan investments, insurance, retirement, and tax, coordinate family finances, and track portfolios — all explainable and within regulatory boundaries.

---

## Phase 4 — Ecosystem & Automation

**Theme:** An open, automated financial operating system.

**Scope:**
- AI Agents (opt-in, strictly bounded automation)
- Automation of routine financial workflows
- Enterprise features
- Open APIs
- International expansion (localized compliance)
- White-label platform
- Community & full Learning Hub

**Value:** WealthWise becomes the operating system for personal finance — an ecosystem where AI agents handle chores under oversight, enterprises and partners build on the platform, and it operates across markets.

**Done when:** Bounded AI agents safely perform routine tasks under user/human oversight; the platform supports enterprise/white-label use and operates in multiple markets; a community and learning ecosystem thrives.

## How phases relate to modules

Every module in [Modules](modules.md) is tagged with its expected phase. The roadmap and module list are kept consistent; if they ever diverge, the [WealthWise Bible](wealthwise-bible.md) is the tiebreaker.

## Roadmap principles

- **Trust-first ordering:** foundations and explainability before automation.
- **Layered, not big-bang:** each phase is usable on its own and sets up the next.
- **Principle-bound:** no phase violates a [business rule](business-rules.md) to ship faster.
- **Evidence-driven:** later phases are shaped by what we learn from earlier ones and from [success metrics](success-metrics.md).
